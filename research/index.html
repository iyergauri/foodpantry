You are being invited to participate in a research study titled Increasing Transparency of Social Resources. This study is being done by Sam Scudder and Gauri Iyer from the University of California - San Diego (UCSD). You were selected to participate in this study because everybody should be aware of the resources they have access to even if they have no intention to use them.

The purpose of this research study is to understand how different marketing strategies affect students’ intentions to visit the Triton Food Pantry. If you agree to take part in this study, you will be asked to complete an quick online survey/questionnaire. This survey/questionnaire will ask about your knowledge of the food pantry and should take less than 30 seconds.

There are minimal risks associated with this research study and participants do not need to provide any information about themselves or others. Research records will be kept confidential to the extent allowed by law and may be reviewed by the UCSD Institutional Review Board.

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you can withdraw at any time by simply exiting the survey. Choosing not to participate or withdrawing will result in no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are entitled. You are free to skip any question that you choose.

If you have questions about this project or if you have a research-related problem, you may contact the researcher(s) at sscudder@eng.ucsd.edu. If you have any questions concerning your rights as a research subject, you may contact the UCSD Human Research Protections Program Office at 858-246-HRPP (858-246-4777).

By clicking “Next” on the survey you are indicating that you are at least 18 years old, have read this consent form, and agree to participate in this research study.
